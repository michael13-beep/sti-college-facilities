About Our Facilities

STI provides different facilities that help students learn, practice skills, collaborate with classmates, and experience a comfortable school environment.

This website presents the major facilities available on the campus. These areas support classroom instruction, technology-based learning, student activities, and practical training. Each facility is maintained to provide students with a safe, organized, and productive learning experience.

Why Facilities Matter

A good learning environment helps students become more confident, focused, and prepared. STI College facilities are designed to support both academic learning and student well-being.

Facility Reminders

Students are encouraged to follow these reminders to keep the facilities clean, safe, and useful for everyone.

Keep classrooms, laboratories, and study areas clean after use.
Use computers, equipment, and school materials properly and responsibly.
Avoid eating or drinking inside computer laboratories unless allowed by school staff.
Report damaged equipment or facility concerns to the proper office immediately.
Respect other students by maintaining proper behavior in shared spaces.
